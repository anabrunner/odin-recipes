Triple Chocolate Cake

Image from Wallpaper Flare

Description

This recipe is for a rich chocolate cake. As the name suggests, we will be using three different chocolates for the perfect decadent treat!

Perfect for a special occasion!

Ingredients

Cake

  • 1.75 cups sugar
  • 1.75 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 5 squares Baker's Unsweetened Chocolate melted
  • 1 cup strong black coffee lukewarm
  • 0.75 cup sour cream
  • 0.25 cup oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Filling

  • 6 squares white chocolate
  • 0.25 cup butter
  • 0.25 cup whipping cream

Garnish

  • Whipped cream
  • 2 squares bittersweet chocolate melted

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350F.
  2. Combine all ingredients for cake in a large mixing bowl and beat on high speed for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour into a greased and floured 9" spring form pan.
  4. Bake at 350F for 45-50 minutes or until toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  5. Cool 10 minutes and then remove sides of pan.
  6. Poke holes over the top of the cake.
  7. Prepare the filling by melting the white chocolate and butter with whipping cream over very low heat. Stir until smooth.
  8. Allow to cool slightly and then pour filling into the holes you poked in the cake. Allow to cool completely.
  9. When the cake is completely cool, garnish with whipping cream and drizzle the melted bittersweet chocolate over the top.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
